Quick Contact

Benefits for you

Collect customer information

When a customer registers with the system you will be able to access their contact information. This includes name, phone, email and address. You can export this information via CSV or use it through the marketing tools we supply.

Market newsletters and promotions

Create newsletters and send them to your customers on a regular basis. Make sure that they are fresh in your mind and know everything that your business has to offer. If you have a promotion on then let them know via email.

Increase sales

Your sales should increase as customers will be more loyal to your business increasing repeat expenditure. You can encourage higher value purchases by including them in offers and fill busy times by creating promotions.

Remind your customers your there

The system can send out notifications when it's the customers birthday which can make a personal impact that reminds them of you. If they haven't purchased from you for a while then the system will send out a 'missing you' email to encourage them back.

Benefits for your customers

One card

Your customer only needs one card to have a loyalty account with any Love Loyalty business. This convenience is possible even though each business has it's own loyalty scheme which doesn't affect the points of other businesses on the system.

Online account

Your customers get there own log in section where they can view the points that they have earnt and the rewards available. They only have one account to view all of the businesses that they are connected to, making it quick and easy.

Latest news on deals and offers

Your customers will be happy to get emailed offers about your business without having to search online. They'll never find a expired voucher that they would have liked to use again.
